About The Skate Project

The Skate Project is a social app attempting to give skaters easier access to skate spots near them, as well as connect them to other members of the skateboarding community. Their mission is to create a platform that has skateboarding, and community engagement at its core. Street skateboarding is an essential part of this culture, and providing an app that exposes this side of skateboarding to everyone will help it flourish. At the same time, skaters are part of a tight-knit community, and this app would provide them with a place to connect with their friends, as well as meet other people wanting to join the sport.
