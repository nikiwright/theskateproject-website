The app will have a map interface that allows users to search for their city or hometown, and all of the street spots in that area would be displayed. Other than directions to those spots, users will be able to see photos, videos, and reviews by other skaters on each skate spot, to know what it looks like and if it's the right place for them. Additionally, users of the app will be displayed on the map depending on the spot they are currently at.
