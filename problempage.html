Street skating has become an incredibly popular sport and activity for audiences of all ages. It's even made it as an event at the Olympics! But skaters shouldn't be reduced to the literal streets to practice their craft.

This is where The Skate Project comes in. An online service that strives to connect skaters to safe, fun, and legit skate spots all over California.
