OUR SOLUTION IS THE SKATE PROJECT
Obviously, our app won't get you to skate like Tyshawn, but the important thing here is to focus on building that skater community! Our application makes it easier for all skaters, from pros to beginners, to connect to each other all across California. As one of, if not the first app to help skaters find spots in their area, we believe this gives us a unique competitive advantage.

APP Interface

The map feature on the app allows users to search for their city or hometown, and have all of the street spots in that area be displayed. The location of different users will be displayed on the map, allowing users to see the popularity of each given spot.

Chat

The chat feature of the app will allow users to talk to each other through private or group chats. This will help skaters meet at spots, get to know each other, and organize group skating events whenever they please.

Engagement Feature

Users are also able to add their own skate spots to the map. Once reviewed, these spots would then be released for the entire community to enjoy. Users will also be able to upload their own pictures, videos, and reviews on every spot, helping other skaters find the perfect place.
