OUR SOLUTION IS THE SKATE PROJECT
Obviously, our website won't get you to skate like Tyshawn, but the important thing here is to focus on building that skater community! Our application makes it easier for all skaters, from pros to beginners, to connect to each other all across California. APP Interface

The map interface feature allows users to search for their city or hometown, and all of the street spots in that area would be displayed. Other than directions to those spots, users will be able to see photos, videos, and reviews by other skaters on each skate spot, to know what it looks like and if it's the right place for them. Additionally, users of the app will be displayed on the map depending on the spot they are currently at.

Chat

The chat feature of the app will allow users to talk to each other through private or group chats. This will help skaters meet at spots, get to know each other, and organize group skating events wherever they please.

Engagement Feature

Users are also able to add their own skate spots to the map. Once reviewed, these spots would then be released for the entire community to enjoy. Users will also be able to upload their own pictures, videos, and reviews on every spot, helping other skaters find the perfect place.
